    Mugendo Sligo

    It's the nearest club to ATU Sligo. Held in the St. Joseph's Church hall, it's a great club for beginners of all ages.

    Kickboxing Collooney

    It's the next nearest club to ATU Sligo. Held in Collooney Parish Hall, it's the perfect club for those who are serious about the sport, students there regularly go to national and european competitions.
